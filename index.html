    Welcome!

    In this site we are providing reconstructed sea surface temperature (SST) satellite images and data, covering the Eastern Mediterranean and Black Sea regions.

    Cloud-free data were produced by applying the DINEOF algorithm over the EUMEDSAT 5 km grid Level 3 data on daily basis, covering 169 hour (seven days) steps.
